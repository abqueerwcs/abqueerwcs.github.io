Board Members

Photo by Kenneth Dillard

🌈 Jason Torchinsky [They/Them] 🌈

President & Treasurer

Jason fell in love with West Coast Swing in 2022 at a local social while interning in Albuquerque, NM—and they’ve been hooked ever since. Back in Madison, WI, they poured their energy into organizing queer West Coast Swing socials through a queer graduate student group at the University of Wisconsin–Madison, helping create spaces where everyone could feel at home on the dance floor.

Jason is one of the founders of ABQueer WCS, and currently serves as our President, Treasurer, and Chair of the Event Planning and Organizing Committee. They travel across the country to compete and bring high-energy hype to every event they attend.

🦋 Sarah Olguin [She/Her] 🦋

Vice President

Sarah began West Coast Swing in 2024 and has been with ABQueer WCS since the very first event. She currently serves as the Vice President and is the Chair of the Advertising and PR commiette (AKA our social media queen). Sarah is a huge advocate for the LGBTQIA2S+ community and she is excited to help our community grow, dance, and be seen.

🦒 Dalton Cole [He/Him] 🦒

Committee Member

Dalton began his West Coast Swing journey in September 2024 after setting a personal goal to learn how to dance. What started with East Coast Swing quickly turned into a love for West Coast Swing after trying it at Southwesties—and it’s been his dance of choice ever since. He especially enjoys the creativity of the dance and the ability to create something beautiful in partnership with others.

Dalton serves on both the Events Committee and the Advertising & PR Committee, and is also the web administrator for ABQueer WCS. He helps coordinate volunteers, plan events, and maintain the organization’s website.

Known for his energetic and goofy presence, Dalton loves hosting events and creating a welcoming, inclusive space where people can feel comfortable, connected, and excited to dance.
