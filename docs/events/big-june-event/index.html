    West Coast Swing Workshops & Queer Prom

    June 27th, 2026 • Albuquerque, NM

    Get Tickets

    ABQueer WCS is hosting West Coast Swing Workshops and a Queer Prom Social Dance!

    Our Workshops feature two tracks: a beginner track focused on fundamentals, and a party track where you can explore fun and unique patterns.

    In the evening, join us for a Queer Prom social dance featuring music from queer artists, slow dances, and all the classic prom vibes.

    What to Expect

    • No partner required
    • Beginner-friendly environment
    • Inclusive and welcoming space
    • Dress however makes you feel your best
