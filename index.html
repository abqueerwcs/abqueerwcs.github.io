ABQueer WCS brings the joy of West Coast Swing to Albuquerque’s queer community — and the joy of queerness to Albuquerque’s West Coast Swing scene. We create safe, welcoming, affirming spaces to dance, connect, and belong.

What is West Coast Swing?

West Coast Swing is a modern, creative partner dance danced to blues, pop, funk, R&B, and more. It’s expressive, playful, and welcoming to all experience levels — no partner required.

Our Values

🌈 Queer-Centered

Our events are for people who identify as queer, those who identify with queer challenges, their loved ones, and community supporters.

🤝 Consent & Safety

We prioritize consent, respect, and care so everyone feels safe, empowered, and comfortable on and off the dance floor.

💃 Accessible Fun

No experience required. Come as you are, learn at your pace, and dance with people who celebrate you.

Ready to Dance?

Whether you’re brand new or a seasoned dancer, ABQueer WCS welcomes you. Check out upcoming events, bring a friend, and join us on the floor.
